Course Structure

Baology follows a regular cadence of six sessions during the week (Lecture Primer, New Concepts, Skill Building, Problem Solving, Case Study, and Homework Review) complementing the main lecture each Saturday. Custom weekly problem sets with live review keep students sharp, and all classes are recorded so you can watch and review at any time. We also give assigned readings each week to be completed before the main lecture and suggested readings for students curious to learn more.
